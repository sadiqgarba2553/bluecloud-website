BlueCloud Technologies is an innovative technology company focused on building next-generation developer tools and infrastructure. We believe that software development should be simple, intuitive, and accessible to everyone.

We created the Flux Programming Language because we were tired of the constant tradeoffs in modern software engineering. We wanted the speed of Rust, the simplicity of Python, and the expressiveness of Kotlin, all in one package.

At BlueCloud, our mission is to empower developers to build incredible software without fighting their tools. Flux is our first step towards that vision.
